Cryptoeconomics · Token Design · Investment Consulting

I am fascinated by how economic systems shape human coordination. Why are we here, and why are we here together?

That question drives my work. I solve cryptoeconomics problems for protocols and institutional investors — the kind that sit at the intersection of mechanism design, market strategy, and governance. Token economies, incentive structures, value accrual frameworks.

I have worked as a researcher, investor, advisor, and builder. That range gives me a perspective most specialists lack: I understand what protocols need to ship, what investors need to underwrite, and where the two fail to communicate.
