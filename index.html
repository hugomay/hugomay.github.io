Hi, welcome to my page.

My name is Hugo and I was born in South Africa. I am fascinated by how blockchain technology, economics and systems engineering intersect.

My work is focused on solving cryptoeconomics problems. That range from those that projects face when designing cryptoeconomies/tokens and how professional investors develop value accrual frameworks for investing in tokens.

Personal interests include Grand Strategy, Game Theory and the extraordinary abilities, but also limitations, of human beings. Why are we here, and why are we here together?

Lemma Solutions – My job

A Cayman‑based consultancy and service provider that helps clients solve the complexities of launching a token. I am responsible for leading engagements that require cryptoeconomics research, token design and go‑to‑market strategies.

Just Cryptoeconomics – My project

We started the project to help the industry make better decisions that relate to tokens, whether it is designing or investing. Just Cryptoeconomics is an open‑access repository of ideas and frameworks. Our product is the Token Fundamentals Dashboard, an analytics tool that helps investors develop a view of the fundamental drivers behind a token’s value.
